Welcome to the Mine Game!

Game Introduction

Dive into an exciting game where luck and strategy come into play. In the Mine Game, you'll navigate through a grid filled with hidden surprises. Your goal is to uncover diamonds while avoiding bombs. Each diamond you reveal adds to your score, but hitting a bomb will end the game!

Game Rules

  • Click on a block to reveal what's inside.
  • There are 22 diamonds and 3 bombs hidden in the X.
  • The more diamonds you find, the higher your score.
  • If you click on a bomb, the game ends immediately.
  • Play smart and try to get the highest score possible!!!
